Soul for a Second

- Cadet Mansur (3298)

 Eyelids feel heavier, tears became dry
Israeli world feels like a chessboard, soul for a piece
Their sun just resigned, as explosion shows their day
And rain stopped, their tears managed it.
Years round into decades, but slaughter carried on
And bloodstains start to vanish: history? never!
Babies' tears burnt the heavens.
Age is just a number, for the devils; may be!
Oasis became a dream, but darkness denies hope
Nature is now just a silence audience
Even knowing his silence is brutal
Kindness withdrawn, ground loves blood!
Vampire's bloody teeth shines - chaos?
Agents! Love your heart, mind your oath
Leave your gun, let the devils flee!
Ocean stopped to roar. Calm and quiet.
Let not the sleeping babies wake up
Once and again. the gloomy palestine air cries
Sympathy
The Almighty! Can't you hear the voices of Gaza?
It is crying for just a blow of heavenly air
Time moves on and Genocide guides him
Orchids lost their colours. withered their beauty.
Meanwhile a terrible beauty in blood.
As chaos leads to calm: just a shell and thousand deaths
Melody aches, Memory Fades. Mind forgets
Only the crying soul weeps forever.
